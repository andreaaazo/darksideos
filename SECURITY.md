## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, do **not** open a public issue or pull request.

Please report it privately by email to: **zorzi.andrea@outlook.com**

Include:
- a clear description of the issue
- affected components or paths
- steps to reproduce
- proof of concept, logs, or screenshots
- potential impact

## Response Timeline

I will make a best effort to:
- acknowledge receipt within 5-7 business days
- validate the report
- coordinate remediation privately before any disclosure

## Scope

This policy applies to security vulnerabilities in this repository and maintained components.

## Disclosure

Please do not disclose vulnerabilities publicly until the maintainers have had a reasonable opportunity to investigate and remediate the issue.
